Privacy Policy

Monggle · Last updated: July 20, 2026

Monggle does not collect personal information. Your data stays only on your device.

Information we collect

Monggle does not collect names, email addresses, locations, contacts, or any other personal information. There are no accounts or logins, and the app contains no advertising, analytics, or tracking tools.

Data stored on your device

Favorite quotes, paper themes, reminder times, and other preferences are stored only on the device where the app is installed. Monggle runs no servers and cannot transmit or view this data. Deleting the app also deletes its stored data.

Notifications

Monggle uses iOS local notifications only when you choose to enable them. Notification content and schedules are never sent to an external server.

Payments

Optional tips are processed by Apple through App Store StoreKit. Monggle never sees or stores card or payment details.

Children's privacy

Monggle is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.
