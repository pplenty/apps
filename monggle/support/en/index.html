Frequently asked questions

How do I add the widget?

Long-press the Home Screen, tap +, search for “Monggle,” and add it to your Home or Lock Screen.

How often do quotes change?

A new line appears each day. You can browse more anytime.

Where are my saved lines?

Lines you heart gather in the Garden tab and stay only on your device.

Do saved lines transfer to a new device?

Monggle has no servers. Your data lives only on your device and will not transfer if you delete the app or switch devices.

Why am I not receiving reminders?

Check iPhone Settings → Notifications → Monggle, then confirm the reminder time inside the app.
