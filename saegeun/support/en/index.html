Frequently asked questions

How do I connect with my partner?

Create a 6-digit code in Settings → Family → Invite Partner. After signing in on your partner’s phone, enter the code under “I’ve been invited — Join with a code” to see the same logs together in real time.

When does a log appear on my partner’s phone?

Usually within seconds. If either phone was offline, pending logs sync automatically as soon as it reconnects.

What happens if I change phones or reinstall the app?

Sign in with the same Apple or Google account and your family and logs will be restored.

Can I import records from BabyTime?

Yes. Export your records from BabyTime as a zip or txt file, then import it from Settings → Data → Import from BabyTime.

How do I delete my account?

Settings → Delete Account removes your account immediately and permanently. If you used Hushabye alone, all family records are deleted. If you share with a partner, shared records remain with the family and only your account is removed.
