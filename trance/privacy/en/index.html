Privacy Policy

Trance · Last updated: July 19, 2026

Your focus data never leaves your device, and nothing Trance collects can identify you.

Data stored on your device

Trance runs no servers of its own. Focus history, the stars in your galaxy, and statistics are stored only on your device. Deleting the app removes all of its on-device data.

Accounts and identifying information

There are no accounts or logins. Trance does not request or collect email addresses, locations, contacts, or other information that identifies you.

Anonymous usage statistics

Trance has no ads or advertising trackers. To improve the app, it uses Google Analytics for Firebase to collect anonymous usage statistics such as which features are used. It is configured not to collect advertising identifiers. A per-device app identifier (IDFV) is used for aggregation, but it is never linked to your focus content or identity.

Notifications

Session completion and daily reminders use local notifications only. Permission is requested only when you choose to enable them.

Payments

The current version is completely free and has no in-app purchases. If paid features are added later, Apple will process payments through App Store StoreKit. Trance will never see or store payment details, and this policy will be updated.

Children's privacy

Trance is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.
