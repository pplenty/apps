Frequently asked questions

Can I leave the app or lock the screen during a session?

By default, yes. The timer keeps running and notifies you when it finishes. With Focus Guard enabled, leaving the app or turning off the screen during a session fades that session's star.

Can I delete a faded star?

Tap the faded star in My Galaxy, then tap the trash icon.

How do I fine-tune the duration?

Turn the timer ring to adjust one minute at a time. One lap is 60 minutes, up to 180. You can also tap the time readout to use the wheel picker.

I can't hear the focus sound.

Choose a sound under Settings → Focus Sound and check your device volume. You can also change it with the speaker button in the upper-right corner of the focus screen.

I can't see the widget or lock-screen timer.

Touch and hold your Home Screen to add the Trance widget. Live Activity appears on the Lock Screen and, on supported devices, Dynamic Island after you start focusing.

Does my data sync across devices?

Focus history and your galaxy are currently stored only on your device. Cross-device sync is not available yet.

Is Trance free?

Yes. Every feature in the current version is free, with no ads.
